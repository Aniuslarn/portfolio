From Seed to Cup

Four steps we refuse to rush

I

Source

We visit farms directly. No brokers. We pay above fair trade and return to the same growers year after year.

II

Roast

Small batches, daily. Each origin gets a custom profile. We never blend to hide a flaw.

III

Rest

Freshly roasted coffee needs 24–72 hours to degas. We wait. Impatience makes bad espresso.

IV

Brew

Temperature, grind, dose, time. Four variables our baristas obsess over so you don't have to.

Our story

Started in a garage.
Still tastes like it.

Dusk Coffee began in 2019 when two friends got tired of bad office coffee and started roasting on a 1 kg drum in a rented garage. They had no business plan, just a shared conviction that coffee deserved more attention than it was getting. Five years later the drum is bigger, the team is twelve people, and the conviction hasn't changed.

"Good coffee doesn't need to announce itself. You'll notice when it's missing."

— Marco V., Head Roaster
