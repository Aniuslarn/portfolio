What you get

Built for people who actually work

Daily focus view

Each morning, Forma surfaces your three most important tasks. Everything else waits. You start with clarity, not a wall of items.

Time blocking

Drag tasks onto your day. Forma shows you where your time actually went, and where tomorrow's plan is already unrealistic.

Auto-carry forward

Unfinished tasks don't disappear. They reappear tomorrow with a small flag. No guilt trip — just a quiet reminder.

Weekly review

Every Sunday, a one-page summary of what you shipped, what slipped, and what that means for next week. No spreadsheet needed.

Linked notes

Add context to any task. Notes live next to the work they describe, not in a separate app you'll forget to check.

Shared workspace

Invite up to 5 teammates. See who's working on what today, without a standup meeting about your standup meeting.
